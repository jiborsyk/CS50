Were They Telling The Truth?

  • Evidence of the Flood
  • Corroborating Ancient Myth

Across the world, humans at the very dawns of their civilization have told eerily similar origin stories. Stories of an ancient flood, stories of an advanced, earlier civilization that had come before them, and provided the seeds to their own civilizations. Were these ancient civiliztions, the Sumerians, Egyptians, and Maya/Olmec truthful in their recounts? How much of myth is rooted in reality?

Why was 11,600-12,800 years ago such an important period in human history?

In recent decades, irrefutable evidence has accumulated which challenges the mainstream accepted story for the genesis of human civilization. New evidence suggests that the biblical flood likely did happen, and there likely was an advanced civilization that existed before the cataclysmic flood.
